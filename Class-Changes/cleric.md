## Core Class Features

### Divine Order (Level 1) - NEW
Choose your playstyle at level 1, independent of domain:

- **Protector:** Heavy armor + martial weapons proficiency (frontline clerics)
- **Thaumaturge:** Extra cantrip + add Wisdom modifier to Arcana/Religion checks (spellcasting clerics)

### Spellcasting (Level 1)
- Prepared spells are now a **fixed number** based on level (not calculated from level + Wisdom modifier)
- Can swap out cantrips when gaining a Cleric level

### Channel Divinity (Level 2)
- Scales to **4 uses** instead of 3

**Divine Spark (NEW):** Versatile option alongside Turn Undead
- Heal OR harm a creature within 30 feet
- Starts at 1d8 + Wisdom modifier, scales with level
- Essentially Cure Wounds at range without a spell slot

**Turn Undead:**
- Undead now get **Frightened AND Incapacitated** conditions (clarification)
- No longer uses their action to Dash (due to Incapacitated)

### Subclass Timing
- **2014:** Level 1
- **2024:** Level 3 (matches all other classes)

### Sear Undead (Level 5) - NEW
Replaces Destroy Undead:
- When undead fail their save against Turn Undead, they take **(Wisdom modifier)d8 radiant damage**
- Can damage multiple undead simultaneously
- Much more useful than the old CR-based destroy mechanic

### Blessed Strikes (Level 7)
- Now a base class feature (was level 8). Choose one:
- **Divine Strike:** +1d8 radiant or necrotic damage to weapon attacks (once per turn)
- **Potent Spellcasting:** Add Wisdom modifier to cantrip damage

### Divine Intervention (Level 10) - REDESIGNED
- Cast any **level 5 or lower spell** without components or spell slots
- Works **once per long rest** (no more failed percentile rolls)
- Can cast Raise Dead/Revivify **without diamond components**

### Improved Blessed Strikes (Level 14) - NEW
- Divine Strike → increases to **2d8**
- Potent Spellcasting → grant **temp HP equal to 2x Wisdom modifier** when dealing cantrip damage

### Epic Boon (Level 19) - NEW
Replaces ability score improvement. Recommended: Boon of Fate.

### Greater Divine Intervention (Level 20)
Can now cast **Wish** using Divine Intervention (must wait 2d4 long rests before using again)

## Major Spell Changes

**New Spells Added:**
- Circle of Power
- Power Word Fortify
- Sunburst

**Healing Spell Buffs (HUGE):**
- **Cure Wounds:** 2d8 + modifier (was 1d8), scales by 2d8 per slot level
- **Healing Word:** 2d4 + modifier (was 1d4), scales by 2d4 per slot level
- **Mass Cure Wounds:** Similar scaling improvements
- **Prayer of Healing:** Also grants benefits of a Short Rest to up to 5 creatures
- **Aura of Vitality:** Healing is now a **free action** (not bonus action) — massively more efficient

**Healing Now Works on Undead & Constructs**
Important for Warforged, Reborn, or undead summons.

**Spiritual Weapon - NERFED:**
- Now requires **concentration**
- Scales better with upcasting
- Cannot stack with Spirit Guardians anymore (most controversial change)

**Conjure Spells Simplified:**
Conjure Animals and similar spells now summon spirits with fixed stats rather than requiring creature stat block lookups.

## Domain-Specific Changes

### Life Domain
- Added to domain spells: Aid, Mass Healing Word, Aura of Life, Greater Restoration
- Removed: Spiritual Weapon, Beacon of Hope, Guardian of Faith, Raise Dead
- **Disciple of Life:** Only applies on the turn you cast the spell (no more Goodberry combo)
- **Preserve Life:** Can now target Undead and Constructs, but only Bloodied creatures
- No longer gets Heavy Armor proficiency by default (use Divine Order: Protector)

### Light Domain
- Added: See Invisibility, Arcane Eye to domain spells
- Removed: Flaming Sphere, Guardian of Faith
- **Warding Flare:** Now works on attacks against you **OR allies** (not just you)
- **Improved Warding Flare:** Replenishes on short rest; grants temp HP to attack target

### War Domain
- Added: Guiding Bolt, Fire Shield, Steel Wind Strike to domain spells
- Removed: Divine Favor, Stoneskin, Flame Strike
- **War Priest:** Can now be used for unarmed strikes; regains uses on short rest
- **War God's Blessing (Level 6):** Spend Channel Divinity to cast Shield of Faith OR Spiritual Weapon without concentration or spell slot
- **Avatar of Battle (Level 17):** Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing damage
- No longer gets Heavy Armor proficiency by default

### Trickery Domain
- **Blessing of the Trickster:** Lasts until end of next long rest; can affect self or others
- **Invoke Duplicity:** Cast as bonus action, no concentration required
- **Trickster's Transposition (NEW):** Replaces Cloak of Shadows

## Key Takeaways

**Added:**
- Divine Order (build flexibility at level 1)
- Divine Spark (ranged heal/harm without spell slots)
- Sear Undead (replaces weak Destroy Undead)
- Improved Blessed Strikes
- Reliable Divine Intervention (no percentile roll)
- Greater Divine Intervention (Wish at level 20)
- Massively stronger healing spells across the board
- Epic Boons at 19

**Nerfed:**
- Spiritual Weapon now requires concentration (can't stack with Spirit Guardians)
- Subclass delayed to level 3
- Various domain spells reshuffled

**Verdict:** The 2024 Cleric is significantly buffed overall. More flexible builds, much stronger healing, and a reliable Divine Intervention. The Spiritual Weapon nerf is the main loss, but War Clerics can still use it concentration-free through War God's Blessing.
