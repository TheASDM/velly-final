## Core Class Features

### Eldritch Invocations (Levels 1+) - MAJOR OVERHAUL
**2014:**
- Level 2: 2 invocations
- Maximum: 8 invocations at level 18
- Pact Boons separate feature at level 3
- One Pact Boon only
- Agonizing/Repelling/Eldritch Spear only worked on Eldritch Blast
- Many invocations required spell slots

**2024:**
- Level 1: 1 invocation
- Maximum: 10 invocations at level 18
- Pact Boons are now invocations
- Can have multiple Pact Boons
- Agonizing/Repelling/Eldritch Spear work on any damage-dealing Warlock cantrip
- Most invocations don't require spell slots

### Pact Magic (Level 1)
- Spell slot progression identical to 2014
- Can swap one cantrip when gaining Warlock level

**Spell List Changes:**
- Added: Thunderclap, Toll the Dead, Bane, Detect Magic, Speak with Animals, Tasha's Hideous Laughter, Mind Spike, Charm Monster, Synaptic Static
- Removed: Shatter, Conjure Fey, Flesh to Stone, Mass Suggestion

### Magical Cunning (Level 2) - NEW
Once per long rest: Spend 1 minute ritual to regain expended Pact Magic slots = half maximum (rounded up)

- Example at level 5: Regain 1 slot (half of 2)
- Example at level 11+: Regain 2 slots (half of 3-4)

### Subclass Timing
- **2014:** Level 1
- **2024:** Level 3

### Contact Patron (Level 9) - NEW
- Learn Contact Other Plane (always prepared)
- Once per long rest: Cast without slot and automatically succeed on INT save

### Mystic Arcanum (Level 11+)
- **2024 adds:** Can replace one Mystic Arcanum when gaining Warlock level

### Eldritch Master (Level 20)
- **2014:** 10 minutes to regain all slots (once per long rest)
- **2024:** When using Magical Cunning, regain ALL slots instead of half (1 minute, once per long rest)

### Epic Boon (Level 19) - NEW
Choose one Epic Boon feat

**Recommended:**
- **Boon of Fate:** +1 CHA (max 30); roll 2d4 and add/subtract from d20 test within 60 ft (once per short rest or when rolling initiative)

### Patron Spell Changes - MASSIVE
**2014:** Patron spells added to Warlock spell list; must choose to learn them

**2024:** Patron spells ALWAYS PREPARED and don't count against spells known

Result: +10 effective spells known (67% increase)

## Pact Boon Changes (Now Invocations)

### Pact of the Blade - MASSIVELY BUFFED
**2014:**
- Level 3 feature
- Create pact weapon as action
- Use STR or DEX for attacks
- Needed Hexblade for CHA attacks

**2024:**
- Eldritch Invocation (can take at level 1)
- Create pact weapon as bonus action
- **Use CHA for attack and damage** (built-in)
- Choose damage type: Necrotic, Psychic, Radiant, or normal
- Weapon counts as magical

**Thirsting Blade (Level 5):**
- Extra Attack with pact weapon
- **Level 12:** TWO extra attacks (3 attacks total)

**Other Invocations:**
- Eldritch Smite (5): 1d8 Force per spell level + knock Prone
- Lifedrinker (9): +CHA necrotic damage on every hit
- Devouring Blade (9): +1d6 damage once per turn; spend Hit Die to heal

### Pact of the Chain
**2024 changes:**
- Can take at level 1 (was level 3)
- Cast Find Familiar at-will (not just ritual)
- New familiar options: Skeleton, Slaad Tadpole, Sphinx of Wonder, Venomous Snake

### Pact of the Tome
**2024 changes:**
- Can take at level 1 (was level 3)
- **Built-in ritual spells:** Learn 2 first-level ritual spells (no separate invocation needed)
- Book can be spellcasting focus
- Can replace cantrips/rituals when gaining level

### Pact of the Talisman - REMOVED
Not in core book

## Subclass Changes

### Archfey Patron - COMPLETE REDESIGN

**Expanded Spell List (Always Prepared):**
- 1st: Misty Step (NEW), Faerie Fire, Sleep
- 2nd-5th: Various (10 total spells)

**Steps of the Fey (Level 3) - NEW:**
When casting Misty Step, choose:
- **Refreshing Step:** You or ally within 10 ft gains temp HP = 1d10 + Warlock level
- **Taunting Step:** Creatures within 5 ft of arrival make WIS save or have disadvantage on attacks vs others

Uses: Prof bonus per long rest OR expend Pact Magic slot

**Misty Escape (Level 6):**
- **2014:** Once per short/long rest; reaction when damaged to turn invisible and teleport 60 ft
- **2024:** Reaction when damaged; teleport 60 ft + invisible + creatures within 5 ft of departure/arrival take 2d10 Psychic (WIS save half); uses equal to Misty Step casts

**Beguiling Defenses (Level 10):**
- **2014:** Immune to Charmed; reflect charm attacks
- **2024:** Immune to Charmed; reaction to halve damage when hit; attacker takes psychic = half damage you took

**Bewitching Magic (Level 14) - NEW:**
- Cast Enchantment/Illusion spells invisibly
- Advantage on CON saves for Enchantment/Illusion concentration

---

### Fiend Patron

**Dark One's Blessing (Level 3):**
- **2014:** Gain temp HP when YOU reduce creature to 0 HP
- **2024:** Gain temp HP when ANY hostile within 10 ft drops to 0 HP (you or ally kills it)

**Hurl Through Hell (Level 14):**
- **2014:** Once per long rest; hit with attack → target disappears until end of next turn; returns for 10d10 psychic (if not fiend)
- **2024:** Expend Pact Magic slot; hit → target makes CHA save; fail = disappears until end of next turn; returns for 5d10 Fire; success = half damage, doesn't disappear; renewable with spell slots

---

### Great Old One Patron - COMPLETE REDESIGN

**Expanded Spell List (Always Prepared):**
Includes Summon Aberration at 4th level

**Awakened Mind (Level 3):**
- **2014:** Telepathy 30 ft, no language needed, always on
- **2024:** Bonus action to form telepathic bond; 30 ft range; 10 minutes duration; must share language; can bond with Prof bonus creatures

**Psychic Spells (Level 3) - NEW:**
- Change any Warlock spell damage to Psychic
- Cast Enchantment/Illusion spells without verbal or somatic components

**Clairvoyant Combatant (Level 6) - NEW:**
When forming telepathic bond: Target makes WIS save; fail = disadvantage on attacks vs you, you have advantage vs them (once per short/long rest OR expend 2nd+ level slot)

**Eldritch Hex (Level 10) - NEW:**
- Always have Hex prepared
- When casting Hex, choose: change one spell damage type to Necrotic OR target has disadvantage on saves against your spells

**Thought Shield (Level 10):**
Unchanged: Resist Psychic damage

---

### Celestial Patron (From Xanathar's)

**Expanded Spell List (Always Prepared):**
- 1st: Aid (NEW), Cure Wounds
- 5th: Summon Celestial (replaces Flame Strike), Greater Restoration

**Healing Light (Level 3):**
- **2024 adds:** Can only restore HP once per turn (prevents abuse)

**Celestial Resilience (Level 10):**
- **2024 adds:** Also triggers on Magical Cunning (mid-adventure temp HP)

**Searing Vengeance (Level 14):**
- **2014:** When YOU make death save; once per long rest
- **2024:** When you OR ALLY makes death save (reaction); adds Blinded to enemies; renewable with 4th+ level slot

## Key Takeaways

**Added:**
- Invocations at level 1
- +2 more invocations (10 vs 8)
- Multiple Pact Boons possible
- Cantrip flexibility (Agonizing/Repelling work on any damage cantrip)
- Magical Cunning (extra slots per day)
- Patron spells always prepared (+10 spells)
- Contact Patron
- Mystic Arcanum swapping
- Pact of the Blade uses CHA (no Hexblade needed)
- Epic Boons at 19
- Better subclasses

**Removed:**
- Subclass at level 1 (now level 3)
- Pact of the Talisman
- Hexblade (not in core book)
- Various invocations (Beast Speech, Beguiling Influence, Book of Ancient Secrets, Eyes of the Rune Keeper)

**Changed:**
- Invocations mostly don't require spell slots
- Pact Boons are invocations (not separate feature)
- Thirsting Blade gives 3 attacks at level 12
- Archfey completely redesigned (teleportation focus)
- GOO completely redesigned (psychic damage specialist)
