# The Covenant Archive

**Tier:** High Quarter &nbsp;|&nbsp; *"Where words become binding"*

The Archive occupies a windowless building of black stone in the High Quarter, its entrance marked only by a simple brass plaque. Inside, gray-robed Archivists maintain Venturia's most important collection: witnessed contracts, binding agreements, and sworn oaths recorded on specially prepared vellum that cannot be altered once the ink dries.

---

## How It Works

Citizens who want their promises to carry legal weight — and in Venturia, that weight is heavier than elsewhere — come here to have their agreements recorded. The Archivists witness each signing with solemn ceremony, speaking words in an old language before applying their seals.

For a higher fee, they'll include certain clauses said to make oath-breaking... inadvisable.

Nobody speaks openly about what happens to those who break Archived agreements. The Archive has never needed to pursue a breach through ordinary courts.

---

## The Deep Archive

The oldest sections, accessible only to senior staff, contain contracts written in languages no living person can read. Some of these documents predate the city itself.

The Archivists maintain them with the same care as recent agreements — suggesting the obligations they record might still be active, whoever or whatever the original parties were.

---

## Character Hooks

- Former Archivist who left the gray robes behind
- Someone bound by an Archived agreement they deeply regret
- Legal scholar who spent years studying the old contracts
- Person who witnessed something they shouldn't have in the deep Archive
- Client whose agreement had unexpected consequences
