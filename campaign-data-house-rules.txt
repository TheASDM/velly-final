THE CRIMSON TAVERN CHRONICLES - HOUSE RULES

= CHARACTER CREATION =
- Standard array or point buy (no rolling stats)
- All official WOTC content allowed, Unearthed Arcana with DM approval
- Start at level 3 (new characters join at party level -1)
- Free feat at 1st level
- Background must tie into campaign world

= RESTING =
- Short rest: 1 hour, maximum 2 per long rest
- Long rest: 8 hours, must be in safe location
- Gritty realism for downtime: short rest = 8 hours, long rest = 7 days in town

= COMBAT =
- Flanking grants advantage
- Drinking potions: bonus action for yourself, action to give to others
- Critical hits: maximize base damage dice, then roll damage dice and add
- Death saves are secret (DM rolls them)
- Revivify: Each use reduces max HP by 1d10 permanently until Greater Restoration

= MAGIC =
- Healing potions: roll or take average, your choice
- Identify: 10 minutes, no spell slot required (ritual only)
- Spell scrolls: Can attempt casting from higher level scrolls with DC 10 + spell level Arcana check
- Counterspell: must identify spell being cast (reaction + Arcana check)

= EXPLORATION =
- Passive Perception used for ambushes/traps
- Active Investigation for searching specific areas
- Travel pace matters: fast (-5 passive Perception), slow (+5)
- Survival checks to avoid getting lost, find food/water, track

= SOCIAL =
- Social skills don't control player characters
- Persuasion/Deception/Intimidation set DC for NPC responses
- Insight reveals emotional state and truthfulness, not mind reading
- Roleplay can grant advantage/disadvantage on social checks

= DOWNTIME =
- Characters can pursue downtime activities between sessions
- Crafting, research, carousing, training, etc.
- See DM for specific rules and costs

= MISCELLANEOUS =
- Inspiration carries over between sessions (max 1)
- Milestone leveling (no XP tracking in session-by-session)
- Loot is distributed however party decides
- Players narrate killing blows in combat
- Rule of Cool: If it's awesome and makes sense, we'll make it work

= DEATH AND CONSEQUENCES =
- Character death is possible and meaningful
- Dead characters can be buried, and their legacy honored
- New characters inherit half the previous character's magic items (party's choice which ones)
- The story goes on
