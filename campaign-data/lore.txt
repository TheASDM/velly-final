THE CRIMSON TAVERN CHRONICLES - CAMPAIGN LORE

= THE WORLD OF AETHERMOOR =

Aethermoor is a world where magic and civilization exist in uneasy balance. The great kingdoms of old have fallen, leaving behind city-states and feudal territories struggling to maintain order in an age of chaos.

= THE AGE OF FRACTURE =

Three centuries ago, the Empire of Aurelia collapsed in a cataclysmic event known as the Sundering. Archmages attempting to harness planar energies tore holes in reality, allowing demons and other horrors to pour through. The resulting wars devastated the continent.

Today, the world is still recovering. Ancient ruins dot the landscape, some containing powerful artifacts, others harboring nightmarish creatures. City-states like Thornkeep have risen from the ashes, but the old empire's glory is just a memory.

= THE DEMON WARS =

Following the Sundering, humanity faced near-extinction from demon incursions. The Order of the Silver Flame, a coalition of paladins and clerics, eventually closed most of the planar rifts and drove back the fiendish armies. However, the Order was destroyed in a final battle at the Shadowfell Gate. Few know what really happened there.

Rumors persist that not all rifts were closed, and that the Crimson Hand seeks to reopen them for some dark purpose.

= THE CRIMSON HAND =

Little is known about the origins of the Crimson Hand. They appeared roughly fifty years ago, operating from the shadows. Their goals seem to involve collecting artifacts from the old empire, conducting blood magic rituals, and destabilizing governments.

Recent evidence suggests they may be trying to complete whatever ritual the old Aurelian archmages began - the very ritual that caused the Sundering.

= THE GODS =

The gods are real but distant in Aethermoor. They grant divine magic to their faithful but rarely intervene directly. Some say they were wounded during the Sundering and have not fully recovered.

Major deities:
- Moradin: God of craft, honor, and endurance (dwarven)
- Pelor: God of sun, healing, and redemption  
- The Raven Queen: Goddess of death, fate, and winter
- Corellon: God of magic, arts, and elvenkind
- Bahamut: God of justice and nobility (dragonborn)

= CURRENT POLITICAL SITUATION =

The region is divided among several city-states and petty kingdoms:

- Thornkeep: Largest city in the region, ruled by the Council of Seven
- Silverpine Confederacy: Alliance of forest villages, led by druid circles
- Ironhearth: Dwarven fortress-city in the mountains
- Ravenshore: Coastal city-state, powerful navy
- The Ashlands: Wasteland to the south, rumors of undead kingdoms

Tensions are rising as resources become scarce and old alliances fray. The Crimson Hand's activities are making everything worse, pushing the region toward war.
