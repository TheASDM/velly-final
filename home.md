# The Codex

The Valley of Shadows Codex is the living record of our campaign — an ongoing dark fantasy chronicle set in the masked city of **Venturia** and the fog-bound valley of **Vallombrosa**.

Browse session recaps, world lore, house rules, and character resources. Everything that happens at the table finds its way here.

> *The fog remembers everything. Whether it tells you is another matter.*
