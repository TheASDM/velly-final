# Welcome to Venturia

*In a city where mirrors show reflections that arrive late, where promises carry weight beyond mere words, and where fog hides secrets older than memory—you have a choice to make.*

Venturia thrives in the shadow of tragedy. For a thousand years, the cursed estates of Vallombrosa have loomed above the city, wrapped in supernatural fog that devours those who venture too deep. The legends are contradictory, the truth buried beneath centuries of embellishment, and most citizens have learned not to think too hard about the prosperity that came after the curse.

But something is changing. Strange occurrences multiply. People disappear. The fog seems restless. And you—whether native-born or newly arrived—find yourself drawn into mysteries that should have stayed buried.

## For Players

<div style="background: rgba(100, 50, 50, 0.1); border: 2px solid #8B4513; border-radius: 8px; padding: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0;">

### 🎭 Campaign Guidelines

**What Your Character Knows:**
- You've grown up with Vallombrosa as part of your landscape—mysterious but familiar
- The legends are contradictory and nobody takes any single version as truth
- Venturia has quirks (delayed reflections, vivid dreams, binding promises) that everyone accepts as normal
- The Autumn Masquerade is the year's biggest event
- People who enter the fog too deeply don't come back

**What Your Character Doesn't Know (Yet):**
- The truth about the ancient tragedy
- Why the fog appeared
- What actually happened to the di Errante family
- Whether any of the legends contain more than a grain of truth

**House Rules & Safety:** [Link to Session Zero Materials](/en/campaign/session-zero)

## Campaign Themes

<div style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(139, 69, 19, 0.1), rgba(70, 70, 90, 0.1)); padding: 2rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 2rem 0;">

This campaign explores:

- **Identity & Recognition** - Who we are when our faces and names can be stolen
- **Memory & Truth** - What happens when the past refuses to stay buried  
- **Promises & Binding** - The weight words carry when magic makes them real
- **Beautiful Wrongness** - Finding hope and connection in a city touched by the uncanny
- **Moral Complexity** - Discovering that victims can be dangerous, and justice is never simple

**Tone:** Romantasy mystery with gothic undertones. Dangerous but not hopeless. Beautiful but unsettling. Intimate character moments matter as much as cosmic stakes.

</div>
